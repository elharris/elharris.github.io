Make Lemonade

  • 1 3/4 C White Sugar

  • 8 C Water

  • 1 1/2 C Lemon Juice

  1. 1 In a small saucepan, combine sugar and 1 cup water.
  2. 2 Bring to boil and stir to dissolve sugar.
  3. 3 Allow to cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate until chilled.
  4. 4 Remove seeds from lemon juice, but leave pulp.
  5. 5 In a pitcher, stir together chilled syrup, lemon juice and remaining 7 cups water.
